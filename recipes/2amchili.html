2am Chili

Description

Absolutely flippin delicious chili recipe that is guaranteed to make you new friends, make a crap ton of it and share it with your new friends, or even neighbors.

Original recipe located here

Ingredients

The Base

  • 1 large can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 normal can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 onion (vidalia)
  • 1.5 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 or 2 cans of light red kidney beans
  • 1 can of green beans
  • 1 can of corn
  • brown sugar
  • cooking oil of your choice

Steps

  1. A pot. Get one.
  2. Groceries, buy them
  3. Did you buy something like McCormick's hot chili seasoning mix?
  4. Cool, now throw that shit away.
  5. WE DO IT LIVE
  6. Spice Mix:
    • Basil leaves: 1 tsp
    • Onion powder: 1 tbsp
    • Chili powder: 4 tsp
    • Cumin: 2 tsp
    • Ground (cayenne) Red Pepper: 1 tbsp
    • garlic powder: 1 tbsp
    • Granulated Sugar: 2 tsp
    • All purpose flour: 2 tbsp
  7. Mix it up right
  8. SHAKE IT
  9. Mmm yeah, smells like touchdowns
  10. Pro note: if your kitchen doesn't have speakers, you are fucking up.
  11. I keeps it classy
  12. Brown that beef. And meanwhile...
  13. CHOP
    • 1/2-3/4 green bell pepper
    • 3-4 cloves garlic
    • Approx 1/2 onion
  14. Open and combine those cans (Be sure to drain the beans and corn first or they'll water down your mix).
  15. When beef is done browning, drain and add it to the pot
  16. Clean the skillet and saute chopped veggies with your oil of choice (we used coconut because we can)
  17. Saute them veggies, preferably like a boss
  18. Add them veggies to the pot
  19. Add that sexy seasoning mix with a few healthy pinches of brown sugar
  20. Finally, add a shot of whatever beer you have on hand
  21. Bring your manly concoction to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer for as many hours as you've got (stir occasionally)
  22. Busy yourself with important things
  23. Until...
  24. READY! CRACKERS! CHEESE!
  25. CONSUME
